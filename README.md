# Ok Study — Admissions & Summer University Camps

Corporate website for a Kazakhstan-based education company helping students apply to universities in the USA, Canada, UK, and EU. 
Also organizes 2–4 week summer study camps on university campuses (English classes + campus life + city excursions).

## What’s inside (MVP)
- Landing page (hero, services, camps, cases, FAQ, contacts)
- RU language first
- Click-to-call / WhatsApp / Email (no form at MVP)
- Deployed on Vercel
